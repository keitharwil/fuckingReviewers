> What is Information Management?

  • Information Management is the strategic discipline focused on leveraging data and knowledge within an organization.
  • It involves the systematic collection, organization, storage, and dissemination of information to support decision-making and operational efficiency.

> Key Aspects of Information Management

  • People: Roles, skills, and governance policies.
  • Processes: Workflows for data handling and lifecycle management.
  • Technology: Databases, analytics tools, cloud systems.
  • Policies: Security, privacy, and compliance frameworks.
