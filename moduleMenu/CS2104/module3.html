Overview

  • This module provides an overview of the typical data science process, from setting a research goal to presenting your findings.
Step 1: Defining Research Goals
Key Outcomes
  • Clear research goal
  • Good understanding of the context
  • Well-defined deliverables
  • Action plan with timetable
Step 2: Retrieving Data
Internal Data
Most companies have existing data collected and stored.
External Data
Data can often be purchased from third-party data brokers or retrieved from publicly available, high-quality sources.
Data Collection
Sometimes, designing a new data collection process might be necessary, though less common for early-career roles.
Step 3: Cleansing, Integrating & Transforming Data
Data Cleansing
Detecting and correcting errors, inconsistencies, and missing values.
Data Integration
Combining data from various disparate sources into a unified dataset.
Data Transformation
Converting data into formats suitable for analysis and modeling (e.g., normalization, aggregation).
Tip
"Garbage in equals garbage out." The quality of your data directly impacts the performance of your models and the reliability of your insights. Thorough preparation is paramount.
Step 4: Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
Visual Techniques
Primarily use graphical methods (charts, plots, dashboards) to make information easier to grasp.
Descriptive Statistics
Calculate summaries like mean, median, standard deviation, and correlations.
Pattern Recognition
Look for trends, relationships between variables, and anomalies.
Note
While the goal isn't cleansing, it's common to discover new anomalies during EDA that might require revisiting prior data preparation steps. Keep an open mind!
Step 5: Building the Models
Predictive Models
Forecasting future outcomes (e.g., sales, stock prices).
Classification Models
Categorizing data into predefined classes (e.g., spam detection).
Insight Models
Understanding underlying relationships within the data.
Tip
Sometimes a combination of simpler models outperforms a single complex one.
Step 6: Presenting Findings & Building Applications
Communicate Impact
Clearly articulate how your findings will change business processes or improve decision-making.
Influencer Role
Convince stakeholders of the validity and value of your analysis.
Automation
For recurring processes, automate the analysis to save time and ensure consistency.
